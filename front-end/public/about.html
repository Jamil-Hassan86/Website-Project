About Us

Our Mission

ShapeShift is dedicated to promoting health and fitness within the South Asian community. Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals of South Asian descent to adopt healthier lifestyles, thereby combatting prevalent diseases commonly found within this ethnic group.

Challenges

We acknowledge the challenges inherent in catering to the diverse South Asian community, including varying languages, cultural traditions, and socioeconomic factors. To address these challenges, we have conducted extensive research and employed a user-centered design approach. By incorporating ethnographic research, surveys, and focus groups, we aim to gain deeper insights into the needs and preferences of our demographic.

Team

Our team consists of dedicated individuals with diverse backgrounds in web development, design, and health sciences. We collaborate closely to ensure that ShapeShift meets the highest standards of quality and user experience.
